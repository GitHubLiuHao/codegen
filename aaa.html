\n Guides
Introduction to Heal Alacrity Tempest Utility Skills and Traits Encounter Guides

Heal Alacrity Tempest is quite a flexible build with a lot of sustain and burst healing, making it a great pick on any fight. The default build is able to provide permanent 25 Might, Alacrity, Fury, Regeneration, Protection, Swiftness and Vigor whilst also extending boons with

Sand Squall
and potentially providing Stability, Aegis and much more with the proper utilities.

Heal Alacrity Tempest has a lot of flexibility in terms of traits, weapons and utilities which can be mixed and matched to suit your needs on any fight. Please understand that this also makes it impossible to summarize all possible builds exhaustively, but we'll try to cover a lot of options so you're able to build your own Heal Alacrity Tempest. If you're looking for more premade builds you can check out this link.

Scepter and Dagger are both excellent choices as a main hand weapon with each having its own unique advantages:

  • Scepter has more healing due to
    Water Trident
     and makes it easier to provide Might to everyone refusing to stand in your
    Overload Fire
    .
  • Dagger has some unique utility like projectile destruction due to
    Ring of Earth
     and mobility due to
    Burning Speed
    /
    Earthen Rush
    .

Whilst you always want to have Water as a primary traitline because it enhances your healing output greatly due to traits like

Soothing Mist
,
Healing Ripple
 and
Aquamancer's Training
 you have a few choices for your secondary traitline:

  • Arcane for even easier access to Regeneration, Protection and Swiftness on top of more healing due to free Concentration and
    Evasive Arcana
    .
  • Fire for a ton of condition cleanses due to
    Smothering Auras
     when combined with
    Powerful Aura
    ,
    Sunspot
    ,
    Unstable Conduit
     and aura shouts.
  • Air for an easier distribution of Fury and Swiftness.

Skills & Traits
Notes

Skills

You're free to fill up the rest of the utility slot with whatever utility you see fit. For a full list of viable utilty skills check the guides.

Traits

Just like with utilities we have quite some choices for our traits/traitlines. For a full list of viable traits and traitlines check the guides.

Rotation

You can skip

Air Attunement
entirely if you need to save
Cyclone
 for a Breakbar Phase.

If you want to stack up more alac as a safety net you can use

Overload Air
when in air, this also allows for an additional
Water Attunement
 dip after you exit
Air Attunement
 to add another additional
Water Trident
 and keep up
Soothing Mist
.

When you are in

Water Attunement
, you can freely use
Water Globe
 or
Tidal Surge
 interchangeably.

Dragon's Tooth
and
Phoenix
 are used to blast our fire fields given by
Wildfire
 and
Overload Fire
. This lets us provide enough Might to people outside of our
Overload Fire
 due to its small range of 180.
